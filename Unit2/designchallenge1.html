Which two websites are you most inspired by?

For my web design portfolio/freelance site, I was inspired by BrightByte Studio and We Are Cuppa

How do they exhibit effective use of space, size or balance?

Both similar in their use of a hero image to learn more about their work, with similar visual hierarchy. They both have a great balance in their use of space and size.

What makes them similiar and different?

Personally, I prefer the lightness of We Are Cuppa, but prefer the segmentation of the BrightByte Studio one page scroll. I like when the navigation goes with you as you scroll down the page, like it does on BrightByte Studio, but I believe the compactness of the other site is ok without it.
