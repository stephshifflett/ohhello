How do they exhibit effective user of space, size, or balance?

    Both Focuse Lab & Typework utlize big (size) hero images above the fold to direct the users attention to their value propostion (focal point). With this established hierachy the two design firms effectively communicate who they are and what they do immediately to the user, thus limiting any unnecessary searching to determine what the sites have to offer.

    The content of both sites demonstrate the effective user of grouping (positive & negative space) to show relatinoships between imates & images. Also, the white space coupled with a clear hiearchy established by the size of heards & content images allows the user to easily scan the sites for information.

What makes them similar and different?

    Typework's log is place top center, while Focus Lab follows convention by placing their logo in the top left corner.
